How to Apply

Step 1

Fill out the application form for MECAT IICS and submit it.

Step 2

After submission, you’ll receive your login credentials at the email address you provided.

Step 3

Click the Login button and sign in using your username and password.

Step 4

Once logged in, you’ll be redirected to your dashboard — complete your application form there.

Step 5

Pay the application fee for MECAT IICS and submit your final application.
